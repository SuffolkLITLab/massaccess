File court forms online during the emergency

Most courts in Massachusetts are closed. This tool offers a way to reach the court amid teh COVID-19 crisis through online court forms and pro se materials for key areas if urgent legal need. Please select a category to get started.

How It Works

Step 1

Choose a category above and select the form you need to fill out.

Step 2

Fill out and submit your form online. It will be delivered to the court.

Step 3

The court will contact you about next steps. Sign in to view your forms.

About the Project

We're operating out of Suffolk Law's Legal Innovation and Technology Lab in cooperation with the Massachusetts Access to Justice Commission's COVID-19 task force. Though we're focused on MA specific content, we're sharing our work on GitHib in the hopes that our efforts can be replicated in other jurisdictions. Learn more...
