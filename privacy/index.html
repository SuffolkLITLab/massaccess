Suffolk University Law School Legal Innovation and Technology Lab ("the LIT Lab") is a non-profit organization based in the Greater Boston Area that provides free legal help to low-income clients.

The website ("apps.litlab.org") and the guided interviews available there are provided as a free service for low-income residents of the state of Massachusetts. By using the service, you accept and agree to the terms of use and privacy policy set forth below.
